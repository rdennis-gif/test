The Source-to-Pay Landscape for Enterprise Manufacturing

A strategic guide to procurement workflows, buyer pain points, and how Jaggaer competes in the $1B+ manufacturing space.

Explore the landscape

1) Source-to-Pay workflow at a glance

Source-to-Pay (S2P) in manufacturing spans strategy, sourcing, contracting, ordering, receiving, invoicing, and supplier performance. CMOs should map messaging to each stage so campaigns feel operationally relevant.

Source

Category analysis, supplier discovery, RFX orchestration, and risk pre-screening for direct and indirect materials.

Contract

Negotiation, legal redlining, obligation tracking, and pricing governance across plants and regions.

Procure

Guided buying, approval routing, PO automation, and receiving controls that reduce maverick spend.

Pay & Analyze

Invoice matching, exception handling, payments, and performance dashboards for supplier, risk, and spend outcomes.

2) Manufacturing customer pain points

Supply continuity risk

Planners need early warning on geopolitical, logistics, and quality disruptions before production lines are impacted.

Fragmented ERP landscape

M&A and multi-plant operations create disconnected workflows, making standardization and visibility difficult.

Slow procurement cycle times

Manual sourcing and approvals delay launches, capex projects, and response to volatile commodity pricing.

4) Strategic narrative framework

  • Business trigger: Margin pressure + supply risk.
  • Operational blocker: Siloed sourcing and AP data.
  • Capability promise: Unified S2P decisions at plant speed.
  • Proof: Faster cycle time, fewer disruptions, better savings realization.
